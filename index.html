Academy for Internet Research

Who are we?

We are a group of security researchers that wish to make internet free, safe and accessible to all.

What do we do?

We specialize in making internet safer by proactively scanning and checking for active vulnerabilities, open ports and CPE strings in the wild against all known ipv4 addresses. If we see an open port we will never do anything malicious and if we suspect you are vulnerable to something nasty send you an email assocaited with your domain. Think of us as future Shodan or Censys working for the benefit of all!

I dont want to make the internet safer ...

We are sorry to hear that. Hope you reconsider .. but if your mind is set please send us an email to abuse@academyforinternetresearch.org with your IP address or ranges that we will promptly blacklist

How can I contribute ...

If you have ideas on how to make the process better or wish to collaborate please send us an email to tech@academyforinternetresearch.org, we are looking forward to hearing from you!
